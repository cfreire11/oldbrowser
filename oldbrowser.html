Your Browser Is Old!!

Your internet browser is not up to date. your internet browser is your application that lets you use the internet: Safari, internet explorer, chrome, SeaMonkey ext. This means there are newer versions of your internet browser that can make your internet surfing experience better. Updating your browser will let you explore the internet faster and use new applications not available on your old browser. It can also better protect you from viruses and malware and give you access to new websites you do not currently have access to. Finally, updating will make you computer less likely to crash or work slowly. If you are a very active, cutting edge computer user, we recommend you update your browser. If you are more casual and do not need the latest internet features, there is no need. If you decide to update, click here for clear instructions to continue.
